Our Journey

A simple timeline of how our team was created

2024
- 100% Done
  • November: 3-person core team established, circulation of initial ideas & brainstorming within the core team happened during this month.
  • December: First programming lead committed to joining. 4-person core team established. Further circulation of ideas and brainstorming continued during this month.
2025
- 0% Done
  • January: 2nd Programming lead committed to joining. 5-person core team established. Talks with EIC initiated for funding & use of facilities. Creation of interest forms & logistics files all began during this month.
  • March: Non-Profit process began. Less activity and attention shown to the team due to increased schoolwork. Creation and finalization of interests forms/flyers continued during this month.
  • April: Non-Profit process continues, fieldwork for interest begins, work on interest forms/flyers continues.
  • May: Non-Profit status acquired. Original interest form sent out via newsletter to 1700+ subscribers. The waiting game begins.
  • June: Successful reach with ~ 20 interested responders. Interview process successfully completed, final roster finalized. Official website created. Funding process ongoing.
