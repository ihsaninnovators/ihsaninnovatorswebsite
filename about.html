About Us

Ihsan Innovators is an FTC robotics team (#30695) based in San Jose, California, founded by 5 teenagers passionate about STEM. We are passionate about innovation, teamwork, and inspiring the next generation of engineers and problem solvers through not only fostering creativity and empowering our team through technology, but also through teamwork and collaboration.

Mission Statement

Our mission as an FTC team is teaching middle and high schoolers necessary STEM skills to succeed in the real world. Our goal is to spark a passion for innovation and technology, to promote creativity and collaboration, all skills that will help prepare our team members for their future.
