About Us

While attending a robotics club at their High School, 5 friends passionate for Robotics and STEM, saw a need for a club where the focus can be on learning rather than a resource to aid with college admissions. They wanted to create an environment helping the young and likeminds to develop technical, leadership, and business skills, fostering creativity alongside team and collaboration. In pursuit of this effort, they researched and found mentors with rich and relevant industry experience who were willing to help them accomplish this goal. This became the foundation of Ihsan Innovators, an FTC team (# 30695), based in San Jose, CA, USA.

Mission Statement

Our mission as an FTC team is to create a stress-free environment for Middle and High School students that can ignite passion for robotics and STEM, empowering teenagers with the skills, confidence, and collaborative spirit to innovate and solve real-world challenges, while fostering a diverse and inclusive community of future leaders and problem-solvers.
