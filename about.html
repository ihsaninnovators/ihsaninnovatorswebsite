About Us

While in their high school robotics club, five friends with a shared passion for STEM saw the need for a team that prioritized real learning. They envisioned a space where young, like-minded students could foster creativity and innovation.

It wasn’t easy to bring this vision to life; they worked tirelessly to find mentors who could bring this dream to life.

This dream is now the Ihsan Innovators, FTC Team #30695, based in San Jose, CA.

Mission Statement

Preparing tomorrow’s innovators for today’s challenges.
